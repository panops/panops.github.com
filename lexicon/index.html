A list of terms specific to the PanOps project, how they are used, and if applicable an example of their use.

Developer Operator
An idividual user and administrator of an installation of the PanOps platform. Typically responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of applications within a PanOps platform environment. Also known as a DO, or a DOer.
DO
see 'Developer Operator'.
edge PanOps
The PanOps codebase at the HEAD of the develop branch, that is, the most recent version of PanOps available.
